Why the Bambu Lab P2S is on every workshop bench

The P2S is Bambu Lab's most popular Core-XY platform — production-grade speed and AI reliability at a desktop price point. Here are the twelve specs that matter.

500 mm/s print speed

Core-XY motion with 20,000 mm/s² acceleration. Production throughput at desktop scale.

256 × 256 × 256 mm

A perfect cube of usable space. Print full-size helmets, jigs and brackets without splitting.

PMSM servo extruder

8.5 kg extrusion force and 40 mm³/s flow rate. Pushes carbon and glass-fiber composites without stalling.

300 °C hotend · 110 °C bed

Engineering-grade thermoplastics: ABS, ASA, PA, PC, PETG-CF — no upgrade kit needed.

AI error detection

Onboard computer vision catches spaghetti and first-layer fails before they ruin a 12-hour print.

1080p · 30 fps camera

HD live stream and time-lapse over the cloud. Monitor every print from your phone.

30-second quick-swap hotend

Toolless changes between 0.2 / 0.4 / 0.6 / 0.8 mm nozzles. Detail prints and bulk on one machine.

5" touchscreen

854 × 480 panel for slicing-free operation. Pick a file from cloud, tap Print.

Adaptive airflow cooling

Variable cooling profile per material — PLA bridges sharp, PETG layers strong.

Textured PEI · hardened nozzle

Flex steel plate releases parts with a twist. Hardened steel nozzle eats abrasives without wear.

Fully assembled

From box to first print in under 20 minutes. Factory-calibrated, no manual leveling.

AMS-compatible

Upgrade later to the Automatic Material System for 4-color and multi-material printing.
